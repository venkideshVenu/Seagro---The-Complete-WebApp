About BikeShare

Welcome to BikeShare, your eco-friendly transportation solution. Discover the convenience of our bike-sharing platform!

Renting a Bike

Rent a bike from any of our stations at ₹15 per hour or ₹100 for a full day. Start your journey by selecting a bike from one of our conveniently located stations.

Note: You can rent up to 4 bikes at once. Clear any outstanding charges over ₹30 before renting a new bike.

Returning a Bike

When you're done, simply return the bike to any station. Your rental fee will be calculated based on your usage time.

Note: A ₹15 repair fee applies for any damaged bikes returned.

Payments

Top up your wallet with any amount up to ₹100 using your card. Rental charges are automatically deducted when you complete your ride.

Easily monitor your wallet balance and transaction history through your personalized dashboard.
